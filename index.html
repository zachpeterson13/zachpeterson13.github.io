About Me


 A second year Computer Science undergraduate student at BYU-Idaho with an interest in developing web applications, mobile apps, and video games. Experience with C++, Java, Android, and a little C#. My love for programming was first discovered during my first web design class when creating my own website. When not working on school projects, time is usually spent doing outdoor activities like hiking, biking, and basketball; playing video games with friends; and learning new things.
